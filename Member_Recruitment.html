About the club

Where curious storytellers become confident creators.

The MSHS Multimedia Club is the school's official student-run creative team — the people behind the photos, videos, graphics, and live coverage you see at every major school event.

Members learn hands-on, working in small crews to plan, shoot, edit, and publish real content for a real audience — no experience required, just curiosity and a willingness to try.

What you'll learn

Eight crafts. One club.

Rotate through specialties or go deep on one — every skill here is one you'll actually use on real school productions.

Photography

Composition, lighting, and candid timing for events, portraits, and campus life.

Videography

Camera handling, shot framing, and coverage planning for live school events.

Graphic Design

Posters, layouts, and social templates using color, type, and layout basics.

Video Editing

Cutting, pacing, sound, and color to turn raw footage into a finished story.

Journalism

Interviewing, reporting, and writing clear, engaging stories on deadline.

Public Relations

Representing the club and school with clear, confident communication.

Social Media Management

Planning posts, captions, and calendars that grow the club's online presence.

Event Documentation

Full-coverage teamwork: planning shot lists and capturing an event start to finish.
